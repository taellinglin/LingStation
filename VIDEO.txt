So...this is my LingStation project for a Metroid Kraid theme remake.
I am using automation lanes for the bass, and custom synthesizers on each track.
Everything you see and hear is synthesized from Rust and math, all done by me.
The original song was on the SNES in Super Metroid, and it is a heavy, tense boss cue.
I wanted to keep that low-end weight and sharp accents while making it my own.
LingStation gives me tight MIDI editing with snapping and note-length tools.
The piano roll lanes let me shape velocity, pan, cutoff, resonance, and MIDI CC.
For sound design, I used an additive and hybrid FM synth, plus a subtractive synth called LingSynth.
Thanks for watching. I'm Ling Lin, and I hope you enjoyed this video about LingStation.
